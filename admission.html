CSA Admission Guidelines

Admission Rules:

  • Admissions open for Grades 6 to 12.
  • Students seeking admission must submit the completed form along with:
    • Copy of birth certificate
    • Recent passport-size photograph
    • Transfer certificate from the previous school
    • Academic report card of the last year
  • An entrance test will be conducted for Grades 9 and above.
  • Parents must accompany the student for document verification.
  • School reserves the right to approve or deny admissions based on merit and seat availability.
  • Original documents must be shown at the time of final admission.
  • Forms submitted without required documents will not be processed.
  • Students must comply with the school’s discipline and attendance rules.
  • Fees must be paid at the time of confirmation of admission.
  • CSA believes in equal opportunity and does not discriminate based on gender, caste, or background.
